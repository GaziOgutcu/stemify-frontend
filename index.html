FAQ
Common questions
What does VocalSplitter actually do? ›
VocalSplitter uses the Demucs AI model to separate any song into two tracks: an isolated vocal stem and an instrumental stem. The free preview splits the first 15 seconds so you can judge the quality before paying.
Why do I need to sign in with Google? ›
Google sign-in lets us tie your splits and payment to your account so you can return to re-download. We only read your name and email — we never post to your account or store anything else.
How long does the preview take to process? ›
Because the free preview is only 15 seconds of audio, most jobs finish in 15–45 seconds. Full-song processing after payment usually takes 1–3 minutes depending on song length.
What audio formats are supported? ›
VocalSplitter accepts MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG, and M4A files up to 50MB. Output stems are delivered as WAV files.
Is there a subscription? ›
No. You pay $3 per song when you want the full download. There are no monthly fees, no credits, and no subscription. If you only ever want the free preview you never have to pay anything.
Is splitting copyrighted music legal? ›
VocalSplitter is a technical tool. You are responsible for ensuring you have the rights to process any file you upload. Splitting music you own or have licensed is fine. Distributing stems from copyrighted songs without permission may infringe copyright law in your jurisdiction. See our legal notice below.
