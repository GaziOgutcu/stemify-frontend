FAQ
Common questions
What is a vocal splitter? ›
A vocal splitter (also called a stem splitter or vocal remover) is an AI tool that separates the individual components of a mixed song — vocals, drums, bass, guitar, etc. — into separate audio files called stems. VocalSplitter uses the open-source Demucs model to do this entirely in the cloud.
How do I remove vocals from a song? ›
Upload your audio file, choose "2 stems", and VocalSplitter will produce a Vocals stem and an Instrumental stem. Download the Instrumental stem to get a version of the song with vocals removed — perfect for karaoke or remixing.
Is it free to use? ›
Previewing your stems is always free. You can listen to every isolated track in your browser before deciding. Downloading a stem costs $3 per file, or upgrade to Pro ($12/mo) for unlimited free downloads.
What audio formats are supported? ›
VocalSplitter supports MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG, and M4A files up to 50MB on the Free plan and 200MB on Studio.
Is splitting copyrighted music legal? ›
VocalSplitter is a technical tool. You are responsible for ensuring you have the rights to process any file you upload. Splitting music you own, music under Creative Commons licence, or your own recordings is generally fine. Distributing stems from commercially released copyrighted songs without permission may infringe copyright law. See our full legal notice below.
How long does processing take? ›
Most 3–5 minute tracks finish in 30–90 seconds. Longer files or more stems (6–10) may take 2–5 minutes. The AI runs on GPU servers so you don't need any special hardware.
